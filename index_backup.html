Program Outcomes

What students walk away with

Not vague promises. Concrete skills they can demonstrate the very next day.

1

Prompt Engineering Basics

Write effective prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Know when to use each tool and how to get accurate, useful responses instead of generic answers.

2

AI-Assisted Study Techniques

Turn any chapter into flashcards, quizzes, and summaries. Use AI to explain difficult concepts in simple language and prepare for exams faster.

3

One Working AI Project

Build a functional chatbot, image generator, or automation tool. Something they can show parents, add to a portfolio, or talk about in future interviews.

4

AI Career Landscape Map

Understand 10+ career paths in AI: prompt engineering, data science, AI ethics, robotics, ML engineering. Know which subjects matter for each path.

5

Critical Thinking About AI

Spot AI-generated misinformation. Understand AI limitations, biases, and when not to trust AI output. Essential skill for the next decade.

6

School-Endorsed Certificate

Official participation certificate co-signed by the school. Recognized proof of AI literacy that stands out in college applications and competitions.

All outcomes are hands-on. Students practice during sessions, not just listen.

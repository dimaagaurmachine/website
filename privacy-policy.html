Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Dimaag aur Machine ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI education program for schools, operated by Amaan Shaikh. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors to our website (futureskills.in), including school administrators, teachers, parents, and students.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1 Information We Collect

a) Information you provide directly

  • Inquiry form: Email address and message content when you submit a question through our contact form.
  • Booking form: Name, email, and scheduling preferences when you book a discussion through our embedded Cal.com widget.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on page, browser type, device type, and general location (city/country level) through standard web analytics.
  • AI voice interactions: If you interact with our AI voice assistant (powered by ElevenLabs), your voice input and conversation data may be processed by ElevenLabs to provide responses.

c) Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect student academic records, grades, or personal identification numbers.
  • We do not collect payment or financial information through this website. All payments, if applicable, are handled directly through the school.
  • We do not collect Aadhaar numbers, PAN, or any government-issued identification.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Schedule and confirm discussion bookings with schools
  • Improve our website experience and content based on usage patterns
  • Send follow-up communication related to your inquiry (we will never send unsolicited marketing)

3 Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may process your data under their own privacy policies:

Cal.com

Used for scheduling discussions. When you book a slot, your name and email are processed by Cal.com.

Formspree

Used to handle inquiry form submissions. Your email and message are routed through Formspree's servers.

ElevenLabs

Powers the AI voice assistant on our website. Voice interactions are processed by ElevenLabs to generate responses. No voice data is stored by us.

Google Fonts

Used for loading website fonts. Google may collect basic connection data (IP address) when fonts are loaded.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services for details on how they handle data.

  • Data submitted through forms is stored with our third-party service providers (Formspree, Cal.com) on their secure servers.
  • We do not maintain a separate database of personal information on our own servers.
  • Our website is served over HTTPS to ensure data transmitted between your browser and our site is encrypted.
  • We retain inquiry data only for as long as necessary to respond to and fulfill your request.

6 Your Rights

In accordance with applicable Indian data protection laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data from our records and third-party services.
  • Grievance redressal: Raise a complaint if you believe your data has been mishandled.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.
